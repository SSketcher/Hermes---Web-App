About project

HERMES is a sport band, which will help you control your health. With HERMES band you can measure your heart rate, temperature and steps.The band is equipped with a display that displays the time and data selected by the user. On this page you can register your product and use all the options offered by HERMES. As a user of page, you can create your profile and view data from sport band in graphic form. If you are interested in the band, please fill out the contact field.
